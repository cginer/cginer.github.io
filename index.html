Hi, I'm Carla!

I obtained my PhD in Genetics in 2018 from Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona. Before that, I completed a a BSc in Biotechnology, where I learned about the complexity of life, and a MSc in Bioinformatics, where I learned how to use computers to study it.

My recent research has been focused on human polymorphic inversions and their role in the evolution of our species, based at Mario Cáceres lab (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona). During my undergraduate years I had some smaller contributions to other projects, the most important one being the characterization of a source of pluripotent stem cells in adults, at Universitat Internacional de Catalunya.
